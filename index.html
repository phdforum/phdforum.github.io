DIAM PhD Forum

The DIAM PhD Forum is a self-organized seminar for PhD students from the Delft Institute of Applied Mathematics at TU Delft. We specifically do not want to organize a (standard) seminar where the audience is lost after five (or ten) minutes. Instead, we want to organize demos on software tools, Q&A sessions with alumni or just talks on how to deal with problems you encountered during the PhD. Of course, we encourage scientific discussion, but the most import thing is: These meetings should be useful and interesting for you!
